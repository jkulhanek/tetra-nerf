Notes

The demo was designed and tested for Google Chrome, but other browsers may also be supported. Please note, that the demo can be intense for older machines and mobile devices. For that reason, we excluded the dense point clouds and tetrahedra from the visualizations. In the demo we wanted to minimise the ammount of data needed to download for the demo and we compressed the colour and depth visualizations using a video compression algorithm. Unfortunatelly, this can cause artefacts to appear in the depth renders. Also, in Google Chrome without hardware-accelerated video decoding you can observe colour flickering.
